Copenhagen: Highlights and Attractions

Project Overview

CMU Communication Design Fundamentals
Author, Illustrator, Traveler
Copenhagen, Denmark
Spring 2015

Opportunity

  • For my commnication design class, we were tasked with created a book with all original content and photos
  • I had the priveldge of traveling to Denmark that semester, and I was really excited to share my favorite attractions and love of exploring new places

The Processs

  • I wanted to portray the ambiance and vibes of Copenhagen
  • I drew from typefaces, colors and styles I saw in the buildings and people around me
  • I started by creating a mood board
  • I iterated constantly on each page, going from a uniform design to each location having its own flavor

My Takeaways

  • This was a really fun project, as there were few parameters or checkpoints
  • I grew a lot as a designer from this, as I learned to explore my own creativity and iterate constantly from my own thoughts
  • I approached this at first as a web design project, making one design that could work for a variety of scenarios, but thanks to my instructor feedback, I was challenged to make each page have a unique feel
  • I gained a lot of insight into how printed media differed from web, furthering my skills as a designer
